Covid - 19


Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and use to recover without requiring special treatment.

To avoid this, we have taken some measures explained below:

Masks

Masks will be given by our cabbin crew to all the passengers to provide a safe flight to everybody.

Distance

Social distance will be maintained all time by the cabin members.

New schedule

It is advised to all passengers to go 45 minutes before to the boarding gate, to avoid queues and possible delays.
