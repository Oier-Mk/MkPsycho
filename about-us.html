About Us

Air is an air transport company which started its activity at the “La Paloma” airport (BIO), better known as Loiu airport in 2013.

This initiative was carried out by Miriam Aguirre and it all started when she realized that Spanish population was willing to travel abroad. Due to the the economic situation after the 2008 crisis, she decided to start with this project making a low cost air company.

Mission: Our mission is simple and easy, it is to provide a pleasant economic flight respecting as much as possible enviroment. 

Vision: We woudl like to have more destinations in order to connect you to more incredible cities. It is mearly important for us to fragment our willings to make them possible so lets be direct, next step: connection with Bangkok.

Values: We provide a thing other competitors doesen't, peace. In our company customer is the main character of our service and we provide everything we have, to provide a pleasant flight.
